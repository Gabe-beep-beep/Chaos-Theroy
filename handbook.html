Welcome to VexTech

We're thrilled to have you on board. Below are some of the essential policies and guidelines to ensure your survival... ahem, success at VexTech.

Core Guidelines

  • Do not press red buttons without written clearance.
  • Report all sentient anomalies to the Department of Spontaneous Engineering.
  • Time travel is not covered by insurance. Proceed at your own risk.
  • All ghosts must be cleared through Paranormal Compliance before entering break rooms.
  • The Boom Floor is off-limits unless wearing approved blast gear.
  • Red Rover sightings must be reported via encrypted channel #R3D-1.

Dress Code

Lab coats are encouraged. Capes are optional. Anything fireproof is recommended.

Emergency Protocols

  • In the event of portal destabilization, do NOT attempt to reason with anything that speaks backwards.
  • Use the buddy system when entering unknown dimensions.
  • Evacuation drills may be indistinguishable from actual events. Treat all alerts seriously.
